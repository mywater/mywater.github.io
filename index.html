Smart Technology

Smart Phones - Smart Water Meters & Smart Banking

My Water cutting edge technology simultaneously shares automatic water meter reading with both Municipalities and Consumers. Speed of access to information, transparency and two way communications being the Cornerstone and Key Drivers.

Mission:
Improve Municipal billing efficiencies, while consumers save water and money without reducing the municipalities current income stream and the need for Government to increase the price of Water. To introduce new technologies with limited disruption to Consumers, Municipal Officials and to not further burden the Poor.
