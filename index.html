Smart Technology

Smart Phones - Smart Water Meters & Smart Banking

My Water cutting edge technology simultaneously shares automatic water meter reading with both Municipalities and Consumers. Speed of access to information, transparency and two way communications being the Cornerstone and Key Driver.

Mission:
Improve Municipal billing efficiencies, while consumers save water and money without reducing the municipality’s current income stream and the need for Government to increase the price of Water. To introduce new technologies with limited disruption to Consumers, Municipal Officials and to not further burden the Poor.

How it Works?

AMR (Automatic Meter Reading) with Web based Data Storage

  • Wireless communication from Meter to Monitor – GSM from Monitor to Web Base to Cell Phone to Call Centre.
  • Two Meter system – Measure to Pay & Measure to Audit
